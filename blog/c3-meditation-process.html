  2. What I thought about meditation before reading chapter 2?

    3. When I had previously attempted meditation, I had found my mind wandered. Chapter 2 in "Search Inside Yourself" discusses that this is very common and it is healthy to keep trying.

    5. Have your eyes shut and then open them slightly when you feel like you are drifting off. I thought it was interesting to focus on bringing back your focus, rather than thinking negatively. Also being conscious of your minds thoughts is important in training it to keep these thoughts out.
